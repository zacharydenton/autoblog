The second beta of WordPress 3.1 is now available!

For things to try, please read our Beta 1 release date announced . A list of known issues is available on our bug tracker.

Already have a test install what you want to switch over to the beta? Try beta tester plugin . Test 3.1 at a test site, not on the active site, such as interaction with plugins that have not been updated can be unpredictable, and we can not predict (see how it works?) If something is broken or not ... that's why we ask people to help us test it!

Tester, do not forget to use the wp-testers mailing list to discuss the errors that you encounter. Plugin and theme authors, please test your plugins for compatibility.
